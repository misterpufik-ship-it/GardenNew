## Why

On mobile, the phone and Telegram quick icons in the header sit a little too far left and too far apart. Tightening their placement improves visual balance without changing navigation behavior.

## What Changes

- Adjust the mobile header quick-link icon group slightly to the right.
- Reduce the spacing between the phone and Telegram icons.
- Keep the existing links, icons, desktop header layout, and mobile menu behavior unchanged.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `mobile-header-quick-links`: Covers the mobile header quick-link layout for phone and Telegram actions.

## Impact
