## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Mobile quick-link icon alignment
The mobile header SHALL display the phone and Telegram quick-link icons slightly right of their previous left-edge position while keeping them within the mobile header's left action area.

#### Scenario: Mobile header quick links are offset right
- **WHEN** the page is viewed on a viewport below 768px wide
- **THEN** the phone and Telegram quick-link group appears slightly farther right than the previous mobile header layout

### Requirement: Mobile quick-link icon spacing
The mobile header SHALL display the phone and Telegram quick-link icons closer together without changing their link destinations or visual icon treatment.

#### Scenario: Mobile quick links are closer together
- **WHEN** the page is viewed on a viewport below 768px wide
- **THEN** the visible gap between the phone and Telegram icons is reduced
